— What we supply

Living produce. Harvested daily.

Every SKU is the output of a specific growing recipe — light spectrum, water cycle, nutrient balance, climate, time. Our living lettuce ships with the root attached, which is why it stays fresh for weeks longer than anything trucked dead from California or Arizona.

— Why partner with SkyFarms

Supply confidence. Brand flexibility.

01 / SUPPLY

Year-round, by design.

365 days a year of consistent volume. No California freight, no climate exposure, no border surprises. Capacity scales with your category growth, container by container.

02 / SAFETY

Food safety as priority one.

Pesticide-, herbicide-, and fertilizer-free by physical isolation. Continuous lot-level traceability. Advised by Dr. Darin Detwiler — featured in Netflix's Poisoned — one of the most respected food-safety voices working today.

03 / LOCAL

Province-of-origin claims that hold up.

Grown in BC. Grown in Alberta. Real provenance, real economics, real provincial pride — and a story your customers care about more every year as tariff and supply-chain volatility rises.

04 / WHITE-LABEL

Your brand. Not ours.

We don't compete with our retail partners on shelf. There is no SkyFarms-branded clamshell in your aisle. You build your category. We build the growing infrastructure behind it.
