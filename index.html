— 02 / The Technology

Modular CEA, engineered for scale.

Plenty, Bowery, and AppHarvest collectively lost more than $2.7B trying to fill billion-dollar warehouses. Our model is structurally different. Each unit is independently economic. Capex scales with offtake, not the other way around.

01 / Grow

Grow.

Hydroponic systems engineered for 98% water recycling. LED spectra tuned per crop. Climate, nutrition, and light controlled to specification.

02 / Operate

Operate.

Modular containers monitored continuously. Yield per tray, energy per kg, days to harvest — every input and output measured and optimised.

03 / Scale

Scale.

Each unit is independently economic. We deploy where the demand is, not where the warehouse fits. Capex scales with offtake.

— 06 / Distribution

We grow the food.
